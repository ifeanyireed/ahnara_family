## Microservices

| Go Service                           | Responsibilities                                                                                                                               | Apps that consume it                                                                                |
| ------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **1. Identity & Family Service**     | Authentication, authorization, family graph, caregiver relationships, RBAC, consent, organizations, hospitals, providers                       | Every single app (Mama, Kids, Provider, Consult, Dispatch, Academy, Circle, Insights, Market, etc.) |
| **2. Health Record Service**         | Unified patient record, FHIR resources, pregnancy records, child growth, immunization, prescriptions, lab results, referrals, clinical history | Mama, Kids, Consult, Provider, Hospital, Emergency, AI, Insights                                    |
| **3. Care Coordination Service**     | Appointments, scheduling, telemedicine, referrals, care plans, home visits, provider availability, queues, notifications, workflows            | Mama, Consult, Provider, Dispatch, Hospital, HomeCare                                               |
| **4. Commerce & Operations Service** | Marketplace, payments, subscriptions, pharmacy, inventory, dispatch logistics, orders, insurance claims, billing                               | Market, Dispatch, Pharmacy, Provider, Hospital, Mama                                                |
| **5. Intelligence Service**          | AI chat, risk prediction, recommendations, multilingual assistant, analytics, reporting, population health, clinical decision support          | Mama, Kids, Provider, Insights, Circle, Academy, Government dashboards                              |
| **6. Engagement Service**            | Chat, communities, notifications, learning, rewards, courses, messaging, campaigns, surveys, reminders                                         | Circle, Academy, Mama, Kids, Provider, Community Health Workers                                     |
